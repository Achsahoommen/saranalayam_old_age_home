Our Inspiring Journey

Saranalayam Old Age Home was born from a powerful vision — to restore dignity, love, and belonging to elderly individuals who have been forgotten by society. In the heart of Chengannur, what began as a compassionate initiative has grown into a sanctuary of hope for many senior citizens.

Our journey began when socially committed individuals witnessed the silent struggles of elderly people who were abandoned, neglected, or left without support. With limited resources but unlimited determination, they built a safe haven where aging would never mean loneliness.

Today, Saranalayam provides 24×7 care, nutritious meals, medical support, emotional companionship, and a peaceful environment. We celebrate festivals, birthdays, and special occasions together — because this is not just a facility, it is a family.

Every resident here carries a lifetime of stories, wisdom, and strength. We are committed to ensuring that their later years are filled with respect, comfort, and meaningful human connection.

Our Vision & Future Expansion Plan

Our vision is to create a future where no elderly person feels abandoned or unsupported. We aim to expand our services and infrastructure to reach more elders in need.

  • Construct additional comfortable living spaces
  • Develop an in-house medical care and emergency unit
  • Introduce physiotherapy and wellness programs
  • Create recreational and spiritual activity centers
  • Launch outreach programs to support neglected elders

With continued community support, we believe these goals will soon become reality.

Founder’s Message

“Saranalayam was built on compassion, not wealth. When I first encountered elders who had no one to care for them, I realized that growing old should never mean growing alone.

This home was created to restore dignity, belonging, and peace. Every smile we see here reminds us that kindness has the power to transform lives.

I invite you to join this mission — through support, volunteering, or simply spreading awareness. Together, we can protect and honor our elders.”

– Founder, Saranalayam Old Age Home
