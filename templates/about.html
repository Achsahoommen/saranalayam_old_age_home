Our Inspiring Journey

Saranalayam Old Age Home was born from a powerful vision — to restore dignity, love, and belonging to elderly individuals who find themselves alone in their twilight years.

Today, Saranalayam provides 24×7 care, nutritious meals, medical support, and a peaceful environment. This is not just a facility; it is a family where every resident is cherished.

Our Vision & Future Plans

Our vision is to create a future where no elderly person feels abandoned. We are constantly working to improve our reach and facilities.

  • Construct additional living spaces
  • Dedicated in-house medical care unit
  • Specialized physiotherapy & wellness programs

Founder’s Message

“Saranalayam was built on compassion, not wealth. When I first encountered elders who had no one to care for them, I realized that growing old should never mean growing alone.”

– Founder, Saranalayam Old Age Home
