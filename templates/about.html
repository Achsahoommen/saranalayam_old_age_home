About Us

Saranalayam Old Age Home is a charitable institution located in Chengannur, Kerala. We provide shelter, care, and dignity to elderly people who are abandoned, neglected, or unable to take care of themselves.

Our Mission

  • Ensure a safe and peaceful living environment
  • Provide nutritious food and medical support
  • Promote emotional well-being and companionship

Facilities

  • 24×7 Care & Support
  • Regular Health Checkups
  • Clean Accommodation
  • Dedicated Staff & Volunteers
