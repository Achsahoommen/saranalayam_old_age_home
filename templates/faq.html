Frequently Asked Questions

An old age home provides housing, care, medical assistance, emotional support, and a dignified living environment for senior citizens who require attention, companionship, and security.

What types of accommodation options are available? +
We provide clean and comfortable shared and private rooms depending on availability. Rooms are well-ventilated, safe, and designed to ensure easy mobility for elderly residents. Basic furniture, bedding, and storage facilities are included.
What care and support services are provided? +
Residents receive daily assistance, medication management, routine health check-ups, nutritious meals, hygiene support, and emotional care. We also arrange hospital visits and emergency medical services when required.
Are there recreational activities for residents? +
Yes. We organize prayer sessions, cultural programs, birthday celebrations, light exercises, storytelling sessions, and social interaction activities to keep residents mentally and emotionally engaged.
How is safety and security ensured? +
The premises are monitored and supervised at all times. We maintain secure entry points, emergency contacts, and staff availability to ensure the safety and protection of every resident.
Are family members allowed to visit? +
Yes. Visitors are welcome during designated visiting hours. We encourage family connections while maintaining privacy and comfort for residents.
How can I donate and support the home? +
You can donate securely through our website. Donations are used for food, medical care, maintenance, staff support, and overall welfare of the residents. Even small contributions make a meaningful difference.
Can I volunteer or contribute in other ways? +
Absolutely. Volunteers can spend time with residents, organize activities, donate essential supplies, or assist in healthcare and administration. Your presence alone brings comfort and happiness to our elders.
